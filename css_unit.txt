CSS UNIT

* we can use length in width, height, margin, padding ad font-size.
* There are two types of unit in css:
    1. absolute unit
    2. relative unit

1. Absolute unit
    cm
    mm
    inch
    px - 1 inch = 96px
    pt - 1 inch = 72px
    pc - 1 pc = 12pt

2. Relative unit
    em :- em is relative to the size of its direct parent.
    rem :- rem is only relative to root(html tag size) note: use to make ratio on phone also.
    vh :- 1% of view port height
    vw :- 1% of view port width
    % :- relative to parent
